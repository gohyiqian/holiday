Trip Itinerary

Singapore to Kunming, Dali, Lijiang, Shangri-La, Daocheng Yading

Itinerary Overview

Duration: 15 Days

Start Date: November 12, 2024

End Date: November 27, 2024

Daily Schedule

Day 1: November 12, 2024

Departure from Singapore at 21:55 hrs, arrive at Kunming at 02:00 hrs on November 13.

Day 2-3: November 13 - 14, 2024

Explore Kunming, visiting Green Lake, Stone Forest, and local markets.

Day 4-5: November 15 - 16, 2024

Travel to Dali by high-speed rail. Explore Dali Ancient City, Three Pagodas, and Erhai Lake.

Day 6-8: November 17 - 19, 2024

Head to Lijiang, visit Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, Lijiang Old Town, and Black Dragon Pool.

Day 9-10: November 20 - 21, 2024

Explore Shangri-La. Highlights include the Songzanlin Monastery, Napa Lake, and Bai Shui Tai.

Day 11-13: November 22 - 24, 2024

Travel to Daocheng Yading, enjoy scenic views, Yading Nature Reserve, and local Tibetan culture.

Day 14-15: November 25 - 26, 2024

Return to Kunming. Final shopping and relaxation before departure at 03:00 hrs on November 27.

Safe Travels & Enjoy Your Adventure!
